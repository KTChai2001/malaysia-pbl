2025 Malaysia Pickleball League Rules & Regulations

Welcome to the 2025 Malaysia Pickleball League! Please review the rules and regulations outlined below to ensure fair play, smooth operations, and an enjoyable experience for all players.

1. SEASON

- Dates will be posted on the Malaysia Pickleball League website.

- Matches can only begin after fixtures and player registrations are submitted.

- League fees must be paid before the season starts to participate.

2. FIXTURES

- Fixutres must be submitted prior to the beginning of the season.

  • Fixtures are the set times and location that your team plays at (home court).

- If a team is unable to book a court, a change of venue/time is allowed at the away team's discretion.

- fixtures are the set times and location that your team plays at (home court).

3. LEAGUES

- Leagues are for Men’s and Mixed Doubles only. Divisions will be formed as necessary by the Committee.

- 1 point awarded per match win. 0 point awarded per match lost.

- Ties in points are resolved by most games won.

- This league operates on a 15-point rally system (serve to win at 14-14)

4. DIVISIONS

- Round-robin home and away matches.

- Each division ideally has 6 or 7 teams.

- Maximum two teams per club in a division (except lowest division).

- Promotion: top 2 teams (1 if fewer than 6 teams). Relegation: bottom 2 (or 1 if fewer than 6).

5. TEAMS

- Teams: 6–12 players.

- Each match has 3 rubbers. Each rubber: best of 3 games to 21 points (serve to win).

Player Nominations

- Clubs must nominate their top 2 players per team, in order of strength.

- Nominated players can't play for lower teams.

- Must play ⅓ of matches or face a 1-point deduction and MYR 50 fine per match missed.

- Replacements allowed mid-season if original player can’t fulfill match quota (Committee discretion applies).

- Changes to nominations must be made 3 days before a match.

Player Eligibility

- Players with 5+ matches in a team cannot play for lower teams (except one level down).

6. ELIGIBILITY OF PLAYERS

- Players must be genuine members of their club.

- All players must be registered before playing.

- Clubs must field appropriate standard players. Committee may penalize violations.

7. PLAY

- Matches follow International Pickleball Federation (IPF) rules.

- Order of play (unless mutually agreed otherwise):

Home Team   Away Team
1,2,3    2,3,1    3,1,2 VS 1,2,3    1,2,3    1,2,3

- Full team lineups must be exchanged before play.

8. TIME OF ARRIVAL

- Start times must be mutually agreed upon.

- 15-minute delays = 1 rubber forfeited. Home team responsible for ensuring matches finish on time.

9. PICKLEBALLS

- Home team provides pickleballs (authorized brand/type).

- Clubs must declare the type of pickleballs on their club form.

10. SCORE CARDS

- Both teams must print or digitally track match scores.

- Cards must be signed by captains and sent to Results Committee within 7 days.

- Late submission = match forfeiture by winning team.

11. POSTPONEMENTS

- Rearranged matches must happen within 28 days or will be voided with no points awarded.

12. CONCEDED MATCHES & INCOMPLETE TEAMS

- A conceded match = fewer than 4 players or failure to show up.

- Incomplete team (under 4 players) gets 1 warning; repeated offenses = MYR 50 fine + potential 1-point penalty.

- Committee may relegate or expel repeat offenders.

13. INJURY

- Retired players = unplayed rubbers scored 15-0 twice.

- Points already scored before retirement are kept.

14. GENERAL

- League Committee has final say and may expel or relegate teams who breach rules.

By participating in the league, teams agree to abide by these rules. Let’s build a thriving pickleball community together! For questions, please contact the league administration.
